User Guidelines

If you want to get the general sentiment about a specific keyword, you can use our Overall Sentiment option. This also, allows you to compare the general sentiment of two different keywords. You can utilize these tools to compare the sentiment of two rivalry keywords that you would like to analyze. Moreover, it also provides a comparative bar chart of the two keywords, which would allow you to better identify the differences among the sentiments of two keywords.

If you prefer to get the sentiment of a specific twitter account regarding various topics, you can utilize our Individual Sentiment option above. Simply click on the Individual Sentiment Analysis above. This option allows the users to put as many keywords as you would like to analyze. Users must put in a specific user account id, for us to analyze the sentiments! It also provides a several informative charts that depicts the historical changes in the length of the tweets, historical changes of the likes and retweets of the tweets regarding the selected topics posted by the selected twitter account.
